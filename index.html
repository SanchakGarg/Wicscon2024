About The Conference

WiCSCon stands as the nation's first Confrence uniquely focused on inclusivity and accessibility within the Computer Science fieldScheduled for April 27, 2024 at Ashoka University. WicsCON is a full-day event that anticipates 200 attendessIt seeks to highlight discussions around inclusive design, intersectionality in tech, career development, innovation through diversity, and foster commpunity building
